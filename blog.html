History of Sloga

Handball club Sloga was founded in 1956, but not under that name. First it was HC "Partizan" and after that changed to HC "Rudar".
I am Jasmina Filipovic and I love handball game,so I decided to create this weblog to write some articles about handball. Here is my email adress: Jasmina

The Sloga was formed in 1956 in Gornji Vakuf.Color of the jerseys are red and white. The club struggled for a long time, until 2015, when the town is getting a sport hall for training. After that moment everything changed for this small club. It finally goes to the first league of the country, which brings a lot of happiness to everyone who lives for handball.
